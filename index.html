About Us

A neighborhood cafe with a warm local pulse

Dan Cafe brings specialty coffee and a relaxed atmosphere to Purok 2 North, Poblacion, Maramag, Bukidnon. It is the kind of place where friends meet over iced lattes, families share pastries, and solo guests can settle in with a favorite drink.

The space is designed to feel modern yet familiar, with welcoming service, cozy corners, and a strong sense of community. Whether you are stopping by for dine-in, curbside pickup, or delivery, Dan Cafe makes every visit feel easy and inviting.
